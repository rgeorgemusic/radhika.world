The Baltic sea meets the Arabian sea in this collaboration between Swedish folk and Indian classical music genres. The melancholic melodies of the Accordion accompanied by the reverberating rhythms of the Tabla creates in the listeners a feeling of oneness with nature. Ljungkrona-George is a collaboration between Larisa Ljungkrona (Gothenburg) and Radhika George (Kerala). The two met at Strisvara - a project that promotes the collaboration of musicians from various spheres of society and various genres of music. The duo debuted in India along with Strisvara playing at various venues and the collaboration continued with the duo working together and performing in Sweden.
